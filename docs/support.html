Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings > Subscription > Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID that made the original purchase.

How do I connect my exchange?

Go to Settings > Linked Accounts and select your exchange. You'll need to create a read-only API key on your exchange and enter it in the app.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use Apple's Secure Keychain to encrypt your API keys, TLS 1.3 for all data transmission, and we never store your exchange passwords. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

What is Paper Trading?

Paper Trading lets you practice trading strategies with $100,000 in virtual funds. No real money is involved, making it a safe way to learn.

Is the AI financial advice?

No. All AI-generated insights, predictions, and analysis are for informational and educational purposes only. They do not constitute financial advice. Always do your own research and consult professionals.
